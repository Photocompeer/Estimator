1. Information We Collect

When you use Pearly's Estimator, we collect data necessary to manage your invoices and measurements. This includes:

  • Account Data: Email address and password via Authentication.
  • Business Data: Company names, contact numbers, and location details.

Your data is used solely to provide the estimation service. We use Firebase Realtime Database to store your records so you can access them across different devices.
